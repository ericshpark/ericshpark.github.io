What this site is

This is the individual portfolio site for the Individual Website deliverable in EST 240. The site hosts three artifacts:

  1. Pocket Authority, the research essay analyzing the visual meaning-making practices of the Mrwhosetheboss YouTube channel.
  2. The Logo, the AI-generated personal mark in three sizes (including a black-and-white variant) plus the AI prompts used to construct it.
  3. The site itself, designed to satisfy CARP (Contrast, Alignment, Repetition, Proximity) and CATN (Content, Aesthetics, Technology, Navigation).

Why Mrwhosetheboss

Among the candidate channels, Mrwhosetheboss offers a particularly clear test case for visual rhetoric in 2026. Arun Maini reviews the same products as Marques Brownlee and Linus Tech Tips, yet differentiates through deliberate mise-en-scène, controlled cutting rhythm, and a calm host persona. He provides a clean laboratory for reading the same set of frameworks (film theory, Chandler's semiotics, and Aristotelian rhetoric) that the course foregrounds.

How the design follows the rubric

Contrast. Serif headlines (Crimson Pro) against a sans body (Inter); navy text on warm-paper background; gold accents reserved for emphasis.
Alignment. A single left-aligned reading column; section titles share a baseline; nav links share an even baseline. Center-alignment is avoided in body content.
Repetition. The same nav bar, footer, and type system on every page; the same color tokens; the same eyebrow style above every page heading.
Proximity. Essay metadata is grouped tightly under the title; logo size labels sit immediately above their tiles; references are tightly clustered at the foot of the essay.

Technology

Hand-written semantic HTML with a single shared stylesheet. No framework dependency. YouTube videos are embedded via official iframe (the channel's Everyday Tech You'll Actually Use appears at the top of the essay page). The logo is SVG so the same file scales from favicon to hero without quality loss. The site is responsive down to ~360px viewports.
